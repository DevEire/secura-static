Meet our Associates

Commercial Lines underwriter

Rylan Bartz

  • State(s): WI, MN, MI
  • Experience: Since 2012
  • Designations: AIC, AU, AINS

Rylan joined the insurance industry right out of college. He held positions at SECURA as a lead business analyst and property claims adjuster before his current underwriter position.

Rylan is proud of his work with two fellow co-chairs at SECURA to run a record-breaking United Way fundraising campaign that raised more than $250,000 for those in need.

When Rylan is not working, he enjoys basketball, golf, and going to his cottage in the summer. He also plays bass guitar in a rock cover band that he and his brother started in 2014.
